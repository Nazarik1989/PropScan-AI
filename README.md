# PropScan AI

The intelligence layer for property documents.

AI-powered property due diligence platform that analyzes property documents, identifies risks, detects missing files and generates structured recommendations for legal professionals and investors.

## Overview

PropScan AI analyzes property-related documents and automatically generates:

- Risk assessments
- Executive summaries
- Missing document reports
- Legal recommendations
- Structured due diligence reports

Instead of manually reviewing lengthy property files, users receive a clear risk breakdown with actionable insights.

---

## Key Features

### AI Risk Assessment

Automatically identifies legal, structural, environmental and transactional risks within uploaded documents.

### Executive Summaries

Generates concise summaries of key findings for rapid decision-making.

### Missing Document Detection

Highlights important documents that are absent from the transaction package.

### Risk Scoring

Assigns an overall risk score based on detected issues and document quality.

### Recommendations

Provides practical next-step recommendations to reduce transaction risk.

### PDF Report Export

Generate downloadable reports for internal review or client communication.

---

## Use Cases

### Property Investors

### Conveyancing Firms

Reduce manual document review time and standardize reporting.

### Due Diligence Teams

Automate the first stage of property risk assessment.

## Workflow

1. Upload property document
2. Extract document text
3. Identify key clauses
4. Assess risks
5. Detect missing documents
6. Generate executive summary
7. Produce recommendations
8. Export report
